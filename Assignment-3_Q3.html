↓↓  Recipes for Making Coffee   ↓↓

    INGRDIENTS:

  • Coffee Powd
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Water

    INSTUCTIONS:

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take ¼ cup instant coffee powder, ¼ cup sugar and 3 tbsp water.
  2. mix well dissolving sugar
  3. using hand beater beat the mixture. you can also beat using whisk or blender.
  4. beat for 5 minutes or until the mixture turns frothy and lightens in colour.
  5. make sure the mixture is thick and creamy. you can store this mixture for a week in an airtight container by refrigerating.
  6. to prepare cappuccino, heat 2 cups of milk.
  7. whisk and get the milk to boil to make the milk frothy.
  8. in a cup, add 1 tbsp of cappuccino mixture.
  9. top with a cup of frothy milk and mix gently.
  10. to make it stronger, add a tbsp of more cappuccino mixture and mix gently.
  11. finally, enjoy cappuccino hot with cookies.
